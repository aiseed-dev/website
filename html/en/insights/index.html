The Climate Change Mistake — CO2 Is Not the Whole Problem

Oil is a material, not just fuel. Renewable energy hardware depends on petrochemicals. Synthetic fertilizers destroyed soil microbes — the largest carbon reservoir. The real solution is soil and ecosystem restoration, not CO2 reduction alone.

Fossil Materials and Modern Civilization — What Comes After Plastic

Modern convenience depends on fossil resources as materials, not just energy. Energy can be replaced, but plastics and medical devices cannot. The post-fossil world requires microorganisms and plants.

The Agriculture Mistake — Chemical Dependency and the Natural Farming Solution

Modern agriculture depends on oil refinery byproducts. One refinery destroyed, and the food system collapses. American agriculture mines topsoil. Corporate farming accelerates fragility. Natural farming is the only resilient path.
