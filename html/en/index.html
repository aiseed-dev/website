With Fable's Release, Building Business Systems In-House Gains the Edge

With the release of Claude Fable 5, Stripe migrated 50 million lines of Ruby in a single day. Just as Nadella says, a business application is essentially a CRUD database with business logic. And Fable can rebuild it — one process at a time — using the running system as the answer key and comparing outputs on the same inputs. Start with read-only reports and queries; with Ruby + Sinatra + raw SQL, drop the middle translation (ORMs and shared classes); and replace the old system without ever stopping it. The advantage in maintaining business systems shifts from vendor outsourcing to in-house development.
