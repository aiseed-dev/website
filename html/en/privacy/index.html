Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

About This Site

aiseed.dev (the "Site") is an individually operated static website that publishes records of natural farming and Light Farming practice, together with structural analysis on food, energy, and AI. The Site does not provide user accounts, login, comments, contact forms, or newsletter sign-ups. No personal information is required to read the Site.

Information We Collect

The Site collects only the following technical information related to browsing.

  • IP address, user agent (browser and operating system), referrer
  • URLs of pages viewed, time spent on page, screen size
  • Approximate geographic location (country / region level — we do not collect addresses or precise coordinates)

The Site does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, payment card numbers, or any other directly identifying personal information through input forms.

How We Use the Information

We use the information collected only for the following purposes.

  • Understanding how the Site is used and improving content
  • Detecting and preventing unauthorized access
  • Maintaining display performance and delivery quality

Cookies and Analytics

The Site uses the following third-party services for analytics. These services may use cookies or similar technologies to collect data.

Google Analytics (GA4)

Provided by Google LLC. The Site uses the measurement ID G-9FLQ963JXM. Google Analytics uses cookies to analyze visitor behavior; IP addresses are anonymized on Google's side. Information collected is handled in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

To opt out of Google Analytics, install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, or block third-party cookies in your browser settings.

Cloudflare

The Site is delivered through Cloudflare's CDN and security services. Cloudflare processes communication logs, including IP addresses, to prevent unauthorized access, accelerate delivery, and detect bots. See Cloudflare's privacy policy for details.

Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share collected information with third parties other than the analytics and delivery infrastructure providers listed above. We do not sell or transfer the information to outside parties, except where required by law.

External Links

The Site contains links to external sites such as Facebook and GitHub. For how those sites handle personal information, please consult their respective privacy policies. We are not responsible for the contents or practices of linked sites.
